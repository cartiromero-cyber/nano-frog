Questions to Ask Your Insurer About Your Roof

A homeowner checklist of questions to ask your insurer or agent about roof age, ACV vs RCV, and documentation. General info, not insurance advice.

Quick answer

Ask your insurer or agent: Is my roof covered at RCV or ACV? At what age does that change? What documentation do you accept to show condition or remaining useful life? Will preservation or a recent inspection affect my policy? Knowing the answers helps you plan before renewal.

What it means

You have the right to understand how your roof affects your policy. A short list of direct questions removes the surprises.

The checklist

  • Is my roof insured at replacement cost (RCV) or actual cash value (ACV)?
  • At what roof age do my terms change?
  • What documentation do you accept to show roof condition or remaining useful life?
  • Does a recent inspection or preservation affect my policy?
  • What would cause non-renewal, and how much notice do I get?

What to watch out for

Get answers in writing where you can, and keep your own documentation current regardless of what any one carrier says.

How Long Does a Roof Last?

How long an asphalt shingle roof really lasts, the factors that shorten it, and how to tell where your roof stands. Inspection required for a real answer.

Quick answer

A typical asphalt shingle roof's serviceable life depends on materials, installation, ventilation, and climate — Georgia's heat, UV, and humidity tend to age roofs faster. Rather than guess from age alone, an inspection and Roof Health Score show how much usable life your specific roof likely has left.

What shortens roof life

  • Intense sun/UV and heat
  • Poor attic ventilation
  • Humidity and algae
  • Storm and wind exposure
  • Installation quality

When it applies

If your roof is in its mid-teens, its condition matters more than its age — which is exactly what the score measures.

What to watch out for

Age is a rough guide, not a verdict. Two same-age roofs can be in very different shape.

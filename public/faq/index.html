The Elytra Lifecycle

What happens after my roof is preserved?

Your complimentary annual reassessment is included — we come back about a year later, re-score the roof, and update your Roof Passport™. No charge, no subscription, nothing to decide on treatment day.

What is the Elytra Continuity Program™?

An optional annual program offered at your year-one reassessment — annual score, updated Passport, photo documentation, storm review, a defined Reliability Reapplication™ allowance, and Preservation Refresh™ price-lock eligibility.

What is a Reliability Reapplication™?

Localized maintenance (up to 2 squares per program year) performed from your annual assessment. Application-related issues are covered separately under our guarantee — and your report states which is which, in writing.

What is the price-lock?

Your assessment locks your quoted price for 12 months — whatever you decide today. Continuous Continuity customers also keep their original pricing for the year-five Preservation Refresh™.

Why do you need my phone and email?

Phone to confirm your assessment window; email to deliver your report and photos. Both are yours to keep, whatever the verdict.

Can I pay for just an assessment?

Yes — a standalone documented assessment is $249 flat (popular with sellers and realtors before listing). Booked homeowner assessments are free.
